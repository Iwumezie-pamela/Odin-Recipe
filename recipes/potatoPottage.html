Potato Pottage

Potatoes are a great substitute for african yams in many recipes and this luscious potato pottage is one of them.

Ingredients

  • Potato of choice. Russet, Idaho or Irish, Yukon, etc.
  • Crayfish Powder (can substitute with fish powder
  • Chicken Bouillon Powder
  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Green Bell Pepper (Optional)
  • Onions
  • Palm oil (can be substituted with vegetable oil)

Steps

  1. Add water to cubed potataoes, turn on heat, add chopped onions, pepper flakes, etc see recipe card for full details. Cook till tender.
  2. Blend red bell peppers, heat up oil and sautee onions
  3. Add blended bell pepper to sauteed onions cook, then add to cooked potatoes.
